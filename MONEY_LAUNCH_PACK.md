## 3. Cleaner approval checklist

Before approving a cleaner into the live cleaner database, check:

- [ ] Full name supplied
- [ ] Phone number supplied
- [ ] Email supplied
- [ ] Postcode supplied
- [ ] Travel radius makes sense
- [ ] Availability supplied
- [ ] Services offered supplied
- [ ] Hourly rate supplied
- [ ] DBS status reviewed
- [ ] Insurance status reviewed
- [ ] Cleaner understands they are self-employed if that is your model
- [ ] Cleaner can log into the Sparkles Cleaner Portal
- [ ] Cleaner can accept/start/complete a test job

Do not approve anyone you would not trust in a customer’s home.

## 5. 7-day money launch plan

### Day 1 — Cleaner supply

- Generate AI Recruitment Autopilot plan.
- Post cleaner advert in 3 local Facebook groups.
- Send WhatsApp referral message to people you trust.
- Aim for 3 cleaner applications.

### Day 2 — Approve cleaners

- Review applicants.
- Approve 1–2 safe cleaners.
- Make sure they can log in.
- Give them one test assignment if needed.

### Day 3 — Customer offer

- Post the customer booking advert.
- Send the customer WhatsApp booking message to warm contacts.
- Ask for one real paid booking from someone you trust.

### Day 4 — First real booking

- Run one booking through the live system.
- Confirm deposit payment.
- Assign cleaner.
- Watch for any confusion.

### Day 5 — Complete the clean

- Cleaner completes the job.
- Final invoice is sent.
- Customer pays balance.
- Ask customer how the process felt.

### Day 6 — Fix only blockers

Only fix problems that affect:

- Booking
- Payment
- Cleaner assignment
- Cleaner login
- Emails
- Customer trust

Do not build new features yet.

### Day 7 — Repeat

- Get the next 2 bookings.
- Keep recruiting cleaners.
- Collect reviews.
- Track profit per job.

## 6. What to measure

Track these numbers weekly:

- Cleaner applicants
- Approved cleaners
- Customer bookings
- Deposits paid
- Jobs completed
- Final balances paid
- Reviews received
- Average booking value
- Cleaner cost
- Profit per booking

## 8. Important safety note

Do not scrape private Facebook, WhatsApp or Indeed profiles.

Use adverts, links and referrals so cleaners apply with consent. That protects your accounts, your reputation and your cleaner database.
