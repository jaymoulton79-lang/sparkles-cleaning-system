- Travel radius
- Hourly rate
- Availability
- Services offered
- DBS status
- Insurance status

## First target

Before taking real customer bookings, aim for:

- 3 cleaners minimum
- 5 cleaners preferred
- 10 cleaners ideal

Each cleaner should cover at least:

- Regular clean
- One-off clean
- Deep clean if possible

Make sure at least one cleaner is available Monday to Friday.

## How to use the cleaner database template

Open `CLEANER_DATABASE_TEMPLATE.csv` and replace the example row with real cleaner details.

Use semicolons for multiple availability days and services, for example:

Availability:

Monday;Wednesday;Friday

Services:

Regular clean;Deep clean;One-off clean

Once the sheet has real cleaners, ask Codex to help add them to Sparkles.
